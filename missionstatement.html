Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide news and information about baseball in the Pacific Northwest to lovers of the sport in the region. In order to accomplish this we will provide up-to-date baseball news articles, statistics, game information, and player information. Like other sports related sites we will features news, information, and multimedia related to players and teams. However, we will have a focus that is less centered on any one team or league in particular, and more on participation and how the sport is enjoyed within the region. This includes news and announcements from high school and amateur leagues regarding game stats and tryout dates - services that have become less available over the years due to declines in small, locally focused newspapers. Obviously coverage of the Mariners fits within the scope of what our visitors will be interested in, but as a fan run website that does not need to approach the team from a marketing or strictly journalistic approach, we will be free to place our own editorial slant to the links and coverage we provide to inject the type of gallows humor Mariners fans have adopted while following arguably the least successful franchise in baseball history who are already off to an abysmal start to the 2017 season.
